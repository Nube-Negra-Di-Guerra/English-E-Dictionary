This dictionary is a free resource, created with the aim of helping users better understand English words. It provides definitions of words, and while it does not include phonetic transcriptions, it offers a simple and easy-to-use interface.

Please note that this dictionary may not cover all English words and may not be suitable for users seeking a very advanced vocabulary experience. We hope you find this dictionary useful and welcome any feedback or suggestions for improvement.
